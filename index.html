Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about our services and approach

What sets us apart from other college counseling companies? +

GradKeys was founded by five current college students and recent high school graduates from Taiwan. Having recently gone through the admissions process ourselves, we understand current trends and the realities students face.

We stay closely connected with admissions counselors at our universities through regular emails, campus visits, and conversations to remain informed on evolving international admissions standards and common application pitfalls.

We put our confidence in you and our service into action by structuring our advising packages around promises. We will guarantee certain outcomes, determined through mutual discussion, and provide a refund if those outcomes are not met.

When should I start working with a college counselor? +

Universities begin evaluating grades and activities from 9th grade, but meaningful preparation often starts earlier. Establishing direction in academics and extracurricular involvement early allows students to build a stronger, more cohesive profile over time. We recommend beginning as early as junior high or before 11th grade to maximize opportunities and strategy.

That said, every student’s situation is different. We offer complimentary consultation sessions for students and parents to assess current preparation and determine the right timing and level of support.

Do you offer SAT/TOEFL tutoring as part of your packages? +

Yes. SAT and TOEFL tutoring can be purchased individually or added to our college advising packages. Our curriculum is tailored to students at different English proficiency levels and academic backgrounds.

For TOEFL, we offer score-based guarantees under agreed-upon conditions and provide a refund if the target score is not achieved.

How does the 20% refund policy work? +

For our Everything package (Rising Senior, Grades 11–12), if you do not receive an acceptance from any of your four target schools and two reach schools (six schools total), we will refund 20% of your package fee.

Specific terms and eligibility conditions will be reviewed during your complimentary consultation. This policy reflects our confidence in our advising approach and commitment to your success.

Can I customize my school list beyond the 8-school package? +

Absolutely. While our Everything Package includes 8 schools (2 safety, 4 target, 2 reach), we understand that each student’s needs and strategy may differ. In fact, all five of our founders applied to more than eight universities! We recognize that broader application plans are sometimes necessary.

Your school list can be adjusted based on your academic profile, goals, and overall application strategy.

What does "unlimited counseling meetings" include? +

Unlimited counseling means you may request meetings at any time through our scheduling calendar, with at least three days’ advance notice. In addition to one required meeting with the student and parents, sessions can be scheduled as needed throughout your package period.

Whether you have questions about your school list, need essay feedback, want to refine extracurricular plans, or require guidance on any part of the application process, we are here to support you without session limits.
