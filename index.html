Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about our services and approach

What sets us apart from other college counseling companies? +

We are current college students and recent graduates from Top 15 universities, making us more familiar with recent admissions trends and better at communicating with and empathizing with students going through the process. We also have strong connections to local high schools in Taiwan, with our founders having graduated from 薇閣 and 康橋, whereas most college counseling companies lack this local connection. Additionally, we offer a 20% refund policy for our Rising Senior package if students don't get accepted to their target and reach schools, demonstrating our confidence in our services.

When should I start working with a college counselor? +

The earlier you start, the better! We recommend beginning in 9th or 10th grade (國九、高一) to maximize your preparation time. This allows us to help with course selection, extracurricular planning, and long-term strategy. However, we also work with rising juniors and seniors, providing comprehensive support tailored to your timeline.

Do you offer SAT/TOEFL tutoring as part of your packages? +

Yes! SAT/TOEFL tutoring is available as an optional add-on to all our packages. Our experienced tutors provide personalized instruction to help you achieve your target scores with proven strategies and customized study plans.

How does the 20% refund policy work? +

For our Rising Senior (高二升高三) package, if you don't receive acceptances from any of your 4 target schools AND 2 reach schools (6 schools total), we will refund 20% of your package fee. This policy reflects our confidence in our counseling approach and commitment to your success.

Can I customize my school list beyond the 8-school package? +

Absolutely! While our Rising Senior package includes 8 schools (2 safety, 4 target, 2 reach), we understand that every student's needs are different. The school list is negotiable and can be adjusted based on your specific goals and circumstances.

What does "unlimited counseling meetings" include? +

Unlimited counseling meetings means you can schedule as many sessions as you need throughout your package period. Whether you have questions about your college list, need essay feedback, want to discuss extracurricular activities, or require support with any aspect of the application process, we're here for you without session limits.
