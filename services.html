What's Included

01

Personalized College List

Building your own right college list is the foundation of your success. With our top-tier counselors, together, you will analyze your academic profile, interests, goals, and preferences to create a balanced list of reach, target, and safety schools. All our counselors, with years of experience, leverage up-to-date knowledge about admission trends, university cultures, and different program strengths so that they can match you to the perfect schools where you can thrive academically, socially, and personally.

  • Complete assessment of the application profile
  • Analysis of personal institutional fit based on balancing personal preferences and drive
  • Strategic balancing of a mix of safety, target, and reach schools
  • Comprehensive insights into admission trends
02

SAT/TOEFL Tutoring

Till today, standardized testing remains an important factor in your application. All our counselors are equipped with knowledge and experience in tutoring standardized testing (SAT / TOEFL), and we will be able to provide you with personalized instructions to help you achieve your target score.

  • Personalized and regular SAT / TOEFL sessions
  • Customized study plans and practice materials
  • Test-taking and time management strategies
  • Regular assessments for tracking progress
03

Essay & Application Review

Your essays are where your authentic voice shines through. We will guide you through brainstorming, drafting, and refining compelling storytelling narratives to showcase your true self. Our editors, equipped with knowledge of what each school wants, will help you craft stories that resonate with the admission committees while remaining your genuine voice through the process.

  • Ultimate brainstorming sessions
  • Line-by-line feedback on drafts
  • Review of supplemental essays and short-answer responses
  • Unlimited editing and revisions on drafts
  • Complete application review for accuracy and consistency
04

Extracurricular Recommendations and Guidance

Extracurricular activities are the most demonstrative way to show your leadership, passion, and commitment. If you’re with us from the very start, we will help you identify opportunities outside of the classroom that align with your interests and strengthen your application. From selecting activities to maximizing their impacts, our experienced counselors will guide you step by step.

  • Personalized activity recommendations based on interests and goals
  • Activity list optimization and presentation
  • Long-term ECL planning and development
  • Referrals to experienced participants and experts in different ECL
05

Waitlist & Interview Preparation

College interviews and waitlist situations require strategic approaches. Through personalized coaching and realistic practice sessions, we help you articulate your story, demonstrate intellectual curiosity, and make a memorable impression. We also provide expert guidance on navigating waitlist decisions and crafting compelling letters of continued interest.

  • Mock interviews with detailed feedback
  • Preparation for common and challenging questions
  • Techniques for showcasing your personality and achievements
  • Waitlist strategy and letter of continued interest guidance
  • Decision-making support for acceptances and deferrals
