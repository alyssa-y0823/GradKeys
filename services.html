What's Included

01

College List Strategy

Building the right college list is foundational to your success. We analyze your academic profile, interests, goals, and preferences to create a balanced list of reach, match, and safety schools. Our counselors leverage deep knowledge of admission trends, campus cultures, and program strengths to identify schools where you'll thrive academically, socially, and personally.

  • Comprehensive assessment of academic credentials and extracurriculars
  • Analysis of institutional fit based on size, location, culture, and programs
  • Strategic balance of reach, match, and safety schools
  • Insight into admission trends and competitive landscapes
02

SAT/TOEFL Tutoring

Standardized test scores remain an important component of college applications. Our experienced tutors provide personalized instruction to help you achieve your target scores. We offer flexible tutoring options tailored to your schedule and learning style, with proven strategies to maximize your performance.

  • One-on-one SAT and TOEFL tutoring sessions
  • Customized study plans and practice materials
  • Test-taking strategies and time management techniques
  • Regular progress assessments and score improvement tracking
  • Optional AP exam preparation
03

Essay & Application Review

Your essays are where your authentic voice shines through. We guide you through brainstorming, drafting, and refining compelling narratives that showcase your unique perspective, values, and experiences. Our editors help you craft stories that resonate with admissions committees while maintaining your genuine voice throughout the process.

  • Brainstorming sessions to identify compelling topics and angles
  • Structural guidance and developmental editing
  • Line-by-line feedback on clarity, impact, and authenticity
  • Review of supplemental essays and short-answer responses
  • Complete application review for accuracy and consistency
04

Extracurricular Recommendations and Guidance

Extracurricular activities are crucial for demonstrating leadership, passion, and commitment. We help you identify meaningful opportunities that align with your interests and strengthen your college applications. From selecting the right activities to maximizing their impact, we provide strategic guidance every step of the way.

  • Personalized activity recommendations based on interests and goals
  • Summer program and competition suggestions
  • Leadership opportunity identification
  • Activities list optimization and presentation
  • Long-term extracurricular planning and development
05

Waitlist & Interview Preparation

College interviews and waitlist situations require strategic approaches. Through personalized coaching and realistic practice sessions, we help you articulate your story, demonstrate intellectual curiosity, and make a memorable impression. We also provide expert guidance on navigating waitlist decisions and crafting compelling letters of continued interest.

  • Mock interviews with detailed feedback
  • Preparation for common and challenging questions
  • Techniques for showcasing your personality and achievements
  • Waitlist strategy and letter of continued interest guidance
  • Decision-making support for acceptances and deferrals
